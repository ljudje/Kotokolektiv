Digitalna orodja ponujajo nove, inovativne in kreativne pristope k oblikovanju tekstilij in oblačil ter tvorjenju širših narativov blagovnih znamk. Erasmus+ projekt Me-You-Us, ki se osredotoča na razvijanje digitalnih 3D kompetenc na področju oblikovanja tekstilij in oblačil, vzpostavlja triletno sodelovanje med tremi visokošolskimi institucijami: Design School Kolding iz Danske, Willem de Kooning Academy iz Nizozemske in Univerzo v Ljubljani. Cilj projekta je razviti izobraževalne module s področja oblikovanja tekstilij in oblačil, ki bodo vključevali različna digitalna orodja, med drugim tudi CLO3D, in bodo odprtokodni. Namenjeni bodo mentorjem, študentom ter drugim zainteresiranim.
Digital tools offer new, innovative and creative approaches to textile and clothing design and the creation of broader brand narratives. The Erasmus+ project Me-You-Us, which focuses on the development of digital 3D competencies in the field of textile and clothing design, establishes a three-year collaboration between three higher education institutions: Design School Kolding from Denmark, Willem de Kooning Academy from the Netherlands and the University of Ljubljana. The project’s goal is to develop educational modules in textile and clothing design, which will include various digital tools, such as CLO3D, and will be open source. They will be intended for fashion educators, students and other interested individuals.
Katedra za oblikovanje tekstilij in oblačil, NTF, UL razvija tri module. Prvi modul, ki izpostavlja sinergijo med konvencionalnim in digitalnim pristopom v oblikovanju tekstilij in oblačil, se je zaključili konec maja 2023. Povezal je štiri predmete, v projekt so bili vključeni študenti prvega letnika.
The Chair of Textile and Fashion Design at the University of Ljubljana is developing three modules. The first module, which focuses on the synergy between conventional and digital approaches in textiles and clothing design, was completed at the end of May 2023. It integrated four courses and involved first-year students in the project.
Študenti so pridobili nova znanja o tem, kako uporabljati digitalna orodja v odpiranju lastne kreativnosti in razvijanju trajnostnih projektov. S pomočjo programa CLO3D so spoznali načine digitalnega modeliranja, virtualnega testiranja, digitalni razvoj oblačil, 3D vizualizacijo in izvoz 2D krojev za fizično izvedbo oblačila. Nastale so digitalne avtorske kolekcije in fizično izvedena oblačila, ki predstavljajo tesno povezavo med realnim in vitualnim.
Students gained new knowledge on using digital tools to promote creativity and develop sustainable projects. With the help of the CLO3D software, they learned about the methods of digital modelling, virtual prototyping, digital garment development, 3D visualisation and export of 2D patterns for the physical implementation of the garment. The outcome was digital original collections and physically produced garments, representing a close connection between the real and virtual worlds.
Erasmus+ projekt Me-You-Us, ki uvaja nove digitalno podprte sisteme izobraževanja na področju oblačilnega in tekstilnega oblikovanja, se fokusira na trajnostni razvoj, raznolikost in vključenost.
The Erasmus+ project Me-You-Us, which introduces new digitally supported clothing and textile design education systems, focuses on sustainable development, diversity and inclusion.

O projektu / about the project
Izbruh pandemije COVID-19 v začetku leta 2020 je močno vplival na modno in tekstilno industrijo, ki se je tako rekoč čez noč ustavila in spremenila življenja ljudi, ki na različnih segmentih delujejo v njeni verigi. Prav tako je močno vplivala na življenje prihodnje generacije oblikovalcev tekstilij in oblačil. Teoretični pouk in praktični študijski proces sta se preselila v spletne učilnice, kar je zahtevalo skoraj nemogoče prilagoditve. Izzivi, s katerimi smo se soočili tako pedagoški delavci kot študenti, so bili nepredstavljivi. Panogi, katere poglavitni značilnosti sta taktilnost in materialnost, je bil onemogočen otip, pogled od blizu in v prostoru, na telesu.
‍
Začetek pandemije marca 2020 je pretresel tudi naše javne predstavitve, zato smo se v duhu časa prilagodili novim razmeram in ustvarili spletno platformo kotofolio.si. Sodobno oblikovana spletna stran širši javnosti v slovenskem jeziku predstavlja najbolj relevantne študentske projekte od študijskega leta 2019/20 naprej, obenem pa omogoča vpogled v izhodiščne koncepte, raziskovanja, razvoj idej in končne produkte skozi fotografije in gibljive slike.

Katedra za oblikovanje oblačil in tekstilij, OTGO,

The outbreak of the COVID-19 pandemic at the beginning of 2020 had a profound impact on the fashion and textile industry, which came to a halt overnight and changed the lives of individuals working in various segments of its supply chain. It also significantly affected the lives of the future generation of textile and clothing designers. The theoretical and practical lessons moved to online classrooms, which demanded almost impossible adjustments. The challenges faced by educators and students were unimaginable. For an industry whose primary characteristics are tactility and materiality, the sense of touch, close-up views and spatial perception were made impossible.
‍
The onset of the pandemic in March 2020 also shook up our public presentations. In the spirit of the times, we adapted to the new circumstances and created the online platform kotofolio.si. The website, in the Slovenian language, presents the most relevant student projects from the academic year 2019/20 onwards to the broader public. Additionally, it provides insight into the initial concepts, research, and development of ideas and final products through photographs and moving images.

Chair of Textile and Fashion Design, OTGO,
